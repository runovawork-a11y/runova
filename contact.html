❓ Frequently asked questions

How do I join a Runova event?

Visit our Runova Events page, choose an event, and click "Join Event". Virtual runs are open to all.

Can I host my own run on Runova?

Absolutely! We welcome event organizers. Use the "Host an Event" button above or email us directly.

Are Runova events free?

Some community runs are free; official virtual challenges have a small registration fee that includes medals & certificates.

How do I get support for an event?

Reach out via our contact form or WhatsApp. We respond within 24 hours.
